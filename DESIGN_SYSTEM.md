# LAS Astronomy NGO - Design System

## Design Philosophy
**Scientific Wonder + Accessibility + Impact**

An astronomy NGO needs to inspire curiosity while maintaining credibility. This design balances cosmic imagery with professional clarity, making space science accessible to all.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Deep Teal** (#0D5F5F) - Trust, science, stability
- **Bright Cyan** (#00D9FF) - Energy, discovery, innovation
- **Warm Gold** (#FFB84D) - Inspiration, achievement, warmth

### Secondary Colors
- **Ocean Blue** (#1E3A8A) - Depth, knowledge
- **Soft White** (#F8FAFC) - Clarity, accessibility
- **Dark Charcoal** (#1F2937) - Professional, readable

### Accent Colors
- **Vibrant Orange** (#FF6B35) - Call-to-action, energy
- **Soft Sage** (#6B9E7F) - Growth, nature, sustainability

## Typography
- **Display Font**: Poppins (Bold, 700-800) - Headings, impact
- **Body Font**: Inter (Regular, 500-600) - Readability, accessibility

## Design Elements
- Clean, minimal layout with breathing room
- Geometric shapes inspired by constellations
- Subtle gradient overlays (teal to cyan)
- Professional shadows and depth
- Smooth, purposeful animations

## Emotional Tone
- Inspiring but grounded
- Professional yet approachable
- Inclusive and welcoming
- Mission-driven and impactful
